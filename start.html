For Banks and Credit Unions

Select a local school and send Account Enrollment Packets home with students.

Ask PTA/PTO to select Volunteers to collect student deposits and deliver them to participating bank branch.

Advertise First Deposit Day CONTEST to students, parents and media to FIRE-UP participation.

Getting Started 529 College Savings Sponsors

Select a local school and send 529 Account Enrollment Packets home with students.

Notify parents to register students at www.schoolsavings.com. Organize PTA/PTO Volunteers to accept deposits.

Create and advertise a First Deposit Day CONTEST to students, parents and the media to increase participation.

Initiate electronic deposit processing with School Savings. CONTACT: 888-787-7728 | support@schoolsavings.com

Allow 2 Weeks for Account Opening and ACH Set-up!
